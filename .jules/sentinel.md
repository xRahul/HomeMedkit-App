## 2024-05-24 - Hardcoded Yandex OAuth Secrets\n**Vulnerability:** The Yandex OAuth Client ID and Secret were hardcoded as plain text strings in `Constants.kt`.\n**Learning:** The application requires these secrets for OAuth integration with Yandex, but hardcoding them exposes them to anyone with source access or who decompiles the APK.\n**Prevention:** Use Gradle's `BuildConfig` to inject these secrets at build time, reading them from environment variables or `local.properties`, ensuring they are not checked into version control.
